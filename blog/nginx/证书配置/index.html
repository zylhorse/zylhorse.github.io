Nginx配置ssl证书

2013-12-11

zylhorse

5分钟

  1. nginx 配置

    $ vim /etc/nginx/config.d

listen 443 ssl;
listen [::]:443 ssl;
ssl_certificate /etc/nginx/fullchain1.pem;
ssl_certificate_key /etc/nginx/privkey1.pem;

  2. 配置生效 $ nginx -s reload
